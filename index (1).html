About North Carolina

A state built for newcomers

North Carolina is one of the fastest-growing states in the United States — a diverse, dynamic state on the southeastern coast with a government structured across 100 counties and a booming economy.

From Charlotte's global banking hub to the Research Triangle's biotech innovation, and from the Blue Ridge Mountains to the Atlantic coastline — NC has it all.
